About Us

My name is Oguzhan Ozdemir, and I founded PupGo after falling in love with my dog Dagsby—my first pet and my best friend. Born in Istanbul, I never had a dog growing up, but adopting Dagsby in 2020 changed everything. He brought joy, loyalty, and love into my life—and made me realize how difficult it can be to travel with a pet, especially internationally.

From navigating complicated airline and country regulations to working with veterinarians and preparing travel documentation, I learned everything the hard way. Eventually, I started helping friends with their pets too. Now, with PupGo, I want to help others—so no one has to leave their furry family behind.

Whether it's domestic or international, we specialize in providing full-service pet travel support. From documentation to transport logistics, PupGo is here to help you and your best friend go wherever life takes you—together.
