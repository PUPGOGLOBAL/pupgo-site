About Us

PupGo was born from a personal journey across continents, inspired by one unshakable bond — the one between a man and his dog.

Our founder, Oguzhan Ozdemir, grew up in Istanbul, Türkiye, always dreaming of having a dog. That dream came true in 2020 when he adopted Dagsby — a stubborn, soulful Boxer who quickly became his best friend and family. The early days were tough, but the love grew stronger with each challenge.

When it came time to travel, Oguzhan realized just how confusing international pet relocation could be — especially for larger dogs. What began as a personal mission to reunite with loved ones without leaving Dagsby behind eventually became a calling.

Today, PupGo helps pet owners navigate every step of the pet travel process — from paperwork to passports, crates to customs. Whether it's a move across the country or across the world, we’re here to ensure your pet gets there safely, comfortably, and by your side.
